# Goblet's Quest

## About the Game
An RPG inspired by 8-bit classics made in RPG Maker MZ, using Javascript.
- A 30 mintue RPG adventure where you play as the monsters of an old-school classic.
- NES-styled pixel art and soundtrack.
- Turn-based combat with three unique party members.

## How to Play
- Arrows/WASD for movement. Z/Enter for confirm. X/ESC for cancel.
- Extended controls & Gamepad controls: https://rpgmakerofficial.com/product/MZ_help-en/01_06.html
            
- To play directly in your browser: https://wingsofathena.itch.io/the-castle-of-frankenstein
- To download, visit releases
